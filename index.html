About Us

Where Learning Meets Creativity

U.S. Memorial Public School is dedicated to providing quality education in a nurturing environment where children learn with creativity and confidence. Located in the heart of Gola, Gorakhpur, our school blends modern teaching methods with strong values.

Following the CBSE Pattern with an English Medium of instruction, we offer classes from Play Way to 8th Standard, ensuring each child receives the best possible foundation for a bright future.

  • ✅ Play Way to 8th Standard
  • ✅ CBSE Pattern Curriculum
  • ✅ English Medium Instruction
  • ✅ Activity-Based Learning
